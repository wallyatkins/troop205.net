Ranks

As you start your journey you need to master requirements.

You must earn your ranks by learning and teaching. First comes scout, tenderfoot, second class, first class, star then life and eagle.

Your leaders and fellow scouts will help you along the way. Patches will be earned upon completion of rank requirements.

Lots of Choices

Explore some of the 138 possible merit badges. Subjects vary from the required 13 Eagle merit badges to Athletics, Science and Ecology, Handicraft, Scoutcraft, Shooting Sports and the Scholastic variety. You have many subjects to explore.

Growth

Leadership is much more than standing in front of the group giving directions. There are four ways every Scout is a leader – First you lead yourself, second you follow cooperatively, third you help your fellow Scouts, and fourth you shape the directions and plans for your patrol and troop.

Giving Back

We volunteer time and talents to help the Virginia Living Museum with their Turtle Census, Virginia Butterfly/Bee Project, Eagle Project participation with brother troop: Troop 200 - Insect shelters, Meals on Wheels, making masks and scrub hats during the COVID closure.
